  • Why is there a star on the upper right corner of each page?

    When you find a page you will want to find again later, you can bookmark it by clicking on the star. The pages you bookmark will be saved for easy access later. Touch the star on the bottom navigation bar and that will open a page with links to your bookmarked pages.

  • Using search doesn’t work for words we commonly use in my language?

    You can help us create a better search. Send us your ideas for search terms and what pages you would expect to find if you searched using that term.

  • Why did I lose my preferences (stars, language or other settings)?

    Sometimes, when your phone runs out of storage space or memory, the phone deletes information like this to gain space. Sorry. Make more space by deleting something else and reset your preferences.

  • My language isn’t listed in the right-hand menu. Why not?

    If you don’t see it, it’s not yet translated. Email us at Hesperian mobile@hesperian.org if you have the time, interest or resources to translate this app into more languages. We can work with you to make it happen and also let you know if a translation is already underway.

  • I want to help make this app better. How can I help?

    By testing the app in communities around the world, we hope to generate suggestions for improvements. Email us at mobile@hesperian.org and we can tell you how you can participate.

    And thank you!
